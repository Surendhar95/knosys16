ANDROID APP DEVELOPMENT

Do you have a need for an Android app that doesn’t exist yet? Would you like to develop that app yourself?
A lot of us have ideas for great apps bouncing around in our brains, but lack the skills necessary to bring our ideas to life.
Only basic knowledge of programming is required for Android App Development, you do not have to be a geek for it! The workshop will start from the basics like designing layouts and building complex layouts. In this course, you will learn to develop applications in the latest and trending OS Android with practical implementation and begin managing your own cool applications. What’s more is that you can even sell out these apps and earn good money.
