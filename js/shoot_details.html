Shoot the Frame

Are you the one who look at a beautiful sunset as a frame? The one in the group who likes to be behind the camera than in front of it? Here is a chance to show your talents by capturing your favourite moment and show it to the whole world.


Themes

  1. Shoot the Face.
  2. Shoot the Land.
  3. Shoot the wild.

Category Definitions

  1. Shoot the Face: Uplifting and inspiring photographs of people. We want portraits and pictures of people at work or play, at home or in a religious setting.
  2. Shoot the Land: Depictions of nature images and landscapes. Landscapes are images of the visible features of an area, including physical elements such as landforms, living elements of flora and fauna, abstract elements like lighting and weather conditions, and human elements like human activity.
  3. Shoot the Wild: Capturing the birds, animals and insects. We want the animal life, plays to be captured.

Entry Conditions

  1. Closing date of all entries : 9th March 2016 midnight 12 pm.
  2. Only college students are allowed to participate.
  3. To enter, you must send the photographs to the email : shoottheframe@gmail.com along with your details(Name, College Name Contact number and E-Mail ID)
  4. Should mention the category to which your photo belongs.
  5. The photo should not have been uploaded either in any site or any competition before and it should be your own click.
  6. It should be of JPEG format and without editing. Only minor burning, dodging and/or colour correction is acceptable, as is cropping.
  7. There is no time or date restriction on when the image was taken. However, we would like to see fresh and exciting photography. Please do not submit copies of previous winners. A previous winner from another contest will be disqualified.

Judging Conditions

  1. Judging will be blind. No names or other indicators are allowed in or on the entry that could indicate who is the author of the entry.
  2. Each entry will be uploaded on Facebook as contestant number and will be judged on the basis of likes.
  3. The judging process is as follows:

    4. LEVEL 1

    The entries with the top 10 highest number of likes till 12th March, 2016 midnight will be taken to level 2 and others will be disqualified.

    LEVEL 2

    The top 10 entries from the LEVEL 1 will be uploaded freshly on Facebook and have to score fresh likes.

    The entries with the top 5 highest number of likes till 16th March, 2016 midnight will be taken to the final level and others will be disqualified.

    FINAL LEVEL

    The top 5 entries will be scored by separate judges.

    The entry with the top 2 highest final score will be declared and awarded with prizes and certificates as first and second , and will be notified through emails and phones.
