Event Format

  1. Event consists of two rounds

  2. First round includes 20 questions to be solved in 30 minutes.

  3. Those who qualify will be given a period of 15 minutes to refresh their skills

  4. Final round will be for one hour, consists of 30 questions

  5. Winners will be the ones who solve maximum number of questions in a minimum amount of time

  2. Use of mobile phones or other electronic media will not be permitted

  3. Any kind of unfair means will lead to disqualification

  4. Participants must carry their respective college identity proof

  5. The judges reserve right to disqualify if any discrepancies are found
