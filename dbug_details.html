D'BUG C'BUG

As a programmer, how often have you been annoyed by the mysterious fiends called bugs. Does the enigmatic 'segment fault' baffle your brain? If you think you have the patience and skill to spot a bug just like an eagle stalks its prey, then D’BUG C’BUG is the one for you !! Let your eyes flow through the code and let your brains discover the hidden bugs.
Get ready to spot the difference between good code and great code!!


Event Format

  1. The event consists of multiple rounds.

  2. This is an individual event.

  3. The participant has to debug a given set of programs and show the required output.

  4. Questions will be in C language.

  5. Questions will be in increasing order of difficulty and so are the marks.

  6. Operating system will be Ubuntu 13.04 . Platform will be GCC compiler. No IDEs will be provided.

  7. Faster you crack the bugs, the more faster you win the race.


Rules

  1. Individual participant has to register for the event

  2. The time limit for this competition will be 2 hours

  3. The number and nature of the problems and the evaluation criteria will be revealed along with the problem statements, at the venue

  4. Use of mobile phones or other electronic media will not be permitted

  5. Any kind of unfair means will lead to disqualification

  6. Participants must carry their respective college identity proof

  7. The judges reserve right to disqualify if any discrepancies are found
