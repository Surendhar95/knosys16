Rules

  1. Each team has 4-5 players.

  2. Teams can register themselves online or on the spot 1 hour before the event starts.

  3. Maximum number of teams allowed to register is 10.

  4. Registrations will be closed when the team count reaches maximum.

  5. Matches for the teams will be conducted as per the schedule given.

  6. A team will have to forfeit game if the players are not present 5 minutes before a match's scheduled start time
