How to reach NITPY

National Institute of Technology, Puducherry (NITPY) is situated in Karaikal. NITPY is located on the National Highway NH45, at about 50 kms from Chidambaram towards south. Bus Routes from Puducherry and Chennai pass through the College to reach Karaikal.
